Shout

Totally random audience suggestions can help push the improvisers skills during rehearsals. I built this little web app to keep you supplied with fresh inputs, while maintaining the flow of the session. You could also use this if the audience seem to be suggesting ideas influenced by what has gone before. Enjoy! Martin Joiner @MartinJoiner

Adjective + Noun

An adjective and a noun are randomly chosen from a list of 1134 and 980 respectively resulting in the potential to create over 1.1 million combinations. Try getting an audience to achieve that level of lateral thinking!
